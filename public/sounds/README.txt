MARS Sound Files
────────────────
Place your alarm sound files here. Required files:

  alarm-default.mp3   — Standard alarm sound
  alarm-gentle.mp3    — Soft, gradual alarm
  alarm-military.mp3  — Sharp, loud alarm
  chime.mp3           — Gentle chime notification

Free sources:
  https://freesound.org (search "alarm", filter by license CC0)
  https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/

File format: MP3, recommended duration 30–60 seconds for looping.
The service worker caches these files for offline playback.
